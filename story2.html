I Could Do This

I have come to believe that I have been blessed with very good people, I think what God didn’t give me in cash he gave me in people. Good people, clever people, kind people, loud loving people, generous people and uber cool people, I have them all and more. When I started toying with the idea of learning programming I asked a few of them what they thought and not a single one tried to talk me out of it. Even the ones I was low key hoping would, didn’t. SMH.

Well what can I say? It really is like learning a new language. HTML, CSS and Javascript are what t I am getting familiar and each has it’s own structure which is referred to as the syntax. They each have different naming conventions and basically what I consider in my mind to be conjugation.

Seeing as my "vocabulary" is growing with each week, it is safe to conclude that just as it is with learning any other language the longer you interact with it the better you get at it. And yes the best way to get good at it is to practice, practice, practice.
